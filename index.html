Diversity & Inclusion | Our Commitment

Statement of Commitment

"At BPCL, we are committed to responsible, sustainable business and upholding human rights. We prioritize dignity, equality, fairness, and inclusion across all our operations. We value our employees, communities, suppliers, and partners, and strive to create an environment where everyone is respected and supported. Our actions reflect our pledge to champion diversity, equity, and inclusion in everything we do."

OUR GUIDING PRINCIPLES

United Nations Guiding Principles (UNGP)

This policy is guided by international human rights standards, including the United Nations Guiding Principles (UNGP) on Business and Human Rights and the core conventions of International Labor Organization (ILO)

International Labor Organization (ILO)

KEY HUMAN RIGHTS AREAS

The Company encourages an inclusive work environment, wherein diversity is valued, and equal opportunities are available to all the employees and stakeholders. The Company follows applicable laws and regulations in the matter of deciding wages/salaries, hours of work and welfare measures. The Company endeavours that the work environment across its operations remain free from discrimination in any form in the matter of compensation, training, opportunities, and employee benefits based on caste, creed, religion, language, ethnicity, disability, age, gender, sexual orientation, race, colour, marital status or union organization or any other status protected by appropriate laws

The Company is committed to treat its employees and stakeholders with dignity and provide a work environment free from all forms of harassment, whether physical, verbal, or psychological. The Company has ‘zero tolerance’ towards and prohibits sexual harassment and/or any conduct that may foster an offensive or hostile work environment, including unwelcome or unsolicited sexual advances.

The Company is committed to provide and maintain a safe, healthy workplace by addressing the risks of accident, injuries, and hazards on a continuous basis. The Company is focused on inculcating a culture of awareness, monitoring and participation surrounding health and safety directly or through agency or contractor as applicable. Company is committed for compensation in case of incapacity or loss of life, to the victim or dependent directly or through agency or contractor, as per applicable policy, regulation or laws. Company is also committed to maintain a workplace that is protected and secured from violence, intimidation, harassment, or any other form of disruptive conditions due to internal or external threat. Employees are encouraged to highlight concerns and suggestions related to occupational health and safety hazards to the Company for redressal. Company envisions a better future by enhancing global sustainability practices. To make this vision a reality, Company has developed its ESG road map following sustainability guidelines on:

  • Environment
  • Social
  • Governance
The company also has a ‘Health, Safety, and Environmental (HSE) Policy’ in place, which makes a commitment to resource conservation, pollution prevention, compliance with all statutory regulations, and the abolition of workplace accidents, diseases, and injuries

The Company respects cultures, traditions, customs, and values of the people in the communities in which it operates. The Company engages with local communities on human rights matters that are important to them such as surface rights, access to clean water, health, and sanitation. The Company engages with people in these communities, including vulnerable and disadvantaged groups. The Company endeavors, within the scope of its capabilities, to promote the fulfilment of human rights through improving the economic, environmental, and social conditions, and further serve as a positive influence at places where it operates for sustainable development of the community at large.

We are committed to maintaining a workplace that is free from violence, harassment, intimidation and other unsafe or disruptive conditions due to internal and external threats. Security safeguards for employees, suppliers and contractors are provided, as needed, and are maintained with respect to privacy and dignity. BPCL has a ‘Security Policy’ in place that commits to maintaining a vigilant and responsible stance, actively preventing instances of theft, misuse, loss, damage, pilferage, and sabotage that could potentially disrupt our business interests and continuity

OUR INITIATIVES & PARTNERSHIPS

OUTSIDE BPCL

Skill Development

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited provides placement-linked vocational training to integrate leprosy-affected and visually impaired youth into mainstream society. It has trained 792 leprosy-affected students and supports 75 visually impaired youth in Latur with skill-based programs, promoting employability, self-reliance, and dignified inclusion.

Community Development

Bharat Petroleum Mumbai Refinery conducts vocational programs for underprivileged youth, women, and persons with disabilities. Under Project Shakti, women SHG members receive bakery and entrepreneurship training in Mahul, Chembur. It also supports visually impaired youth through residential training in acupressure and massage therapy, promoting self-employment.

Employee Voluntary Program

Bharat Petroleum Corporation Limited runs volunteering initiatives for children with autism and developmental disabilities, building family support networks. Volunteers organize and assist in Sports Day events—setting up venues, facilitating games, and supporting children in activities like relay races, 100-meter runs, and drills.
